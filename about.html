About CraftBridge

CraftBridge is an online platform dedicated to supporting traditional craftsmen and handmade artists across India. We act as a bridge between skilled artisans and modern consumers who value authenticity and sustainability.

Our mission is to preserve cultural heritage while providing artisans with fair income opportunities. By eliminating unnecessary middlemen, we ensure better pricing for both creators and customers.

Every product on our platform carries a story — a story of tradition, dedication, and craftsmanship passed down through generations.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable digital marketplace that empowers artisans, promotes ethical consumption, and celebrates handmade excellence globally.
